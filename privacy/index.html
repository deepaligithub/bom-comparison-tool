Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

We do not collect your personal data. BOM Compare Tool does not send your information to our servers or to any third party. Everything stays on your device.

We do not collect data

We do not collect, store, or process your personal data on our servers. We do not collect usage data, analytics, or telemetry. We do not track how you use the app.

Where your data stays

All processing happens on your own machine. The app uses only local storage on your device:

  • BOM files you upload (CSV, Excel, JSON, PLMXML) are used only for the comparison and stored temporarily on your device.
  • Login credentials (username and hashed password) are stored locally for access control only.
  • Mappings and presets you create are saved locally on your device.
  • Logs (if any) are written only on your device.

We do not receive, see, or have access to your files, credentials, or any other data.

4. Your choices

You can stop using the app at any time. Data stored by the app (uploads, logs, local config) remains on your device; you can delete it by removing the app’s data folder or uninstalling the app.

5. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will be revised when we do. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
