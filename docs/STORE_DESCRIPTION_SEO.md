## Full description (Store – “Description” / “Full description”)

Copy the block below into Partner Center as the full app description.

```
Compare two Bills of Material (BOM) files in seconds—CSV, Excel, JSON, or PLMXML. Built for engineers, procurement, and manufacturing teams who need to validate BOMs, spot missing parts, and align column mappings without sending data to the cloud.

WHAT IT DOES
• Compare BOM A vs BOM B side by side
• Support for CSV, Excel (.xlsx), JSON, and PLMXML
• Automatic or custom column mapping (part numbers, descriptions, quantities, etc.)
• See matched rows, missing-in-A, missing-in-B, and quantity/field differences
• Export results to CSV or PDF (paid)
• Save and reuse mapping presets (paid)
• All processing on your PC—your BOM data never leaves your device

WHO IT’S FOR
• Manufacturing and supply chain teams
• Engineering and PLM users
• Procurement and quality checking
• Anyone comparing bills of materials, part lists, or BoM files from different sources

WHY BOM COMPARE TOOL
• No cloud upload—compare sensitive BOMs locally
• No account required to try (default login: admin/admin)
• Handles large files; optional pagination for big result sets
• Simple UI: upload two files, pick or confirm mapping, view results

Download BOM Compare Tool to compare bills of material quickly and keep your data on your machine. Free tier includes full compare and view; upgrade for export and mapping manager.
```
